Marenthyu's Guide to Custom Firmware using SeedMiner

Required Reading

Welcome! You have come here, i guess, on the prospect of installing custom Firmware on your (new) Nintendo 3DS using the single-System Nintendo DSiWare Injection; Meaning you do not have:

  • a friend with Custom Firmware willing to transfer their CFW to your device
  • a supported Flashcard
and are on Firmware Version 11.4 or newer. (The newest one currently being 11.6.0)
WARNING! This guide is only tested on 11.4 through 11.6 - if you are on a newer version and see this warning, i take no responsibility for your Device bricking as the used tools are only confirmed working with 11.6 (at the time of writing this)

If you do have any of the above and or are not on 11.4 through 11.6, please head over to 3ds.hacks.guide and follow it, especially if you are on an earlier Version, as there are WAY faster methods for older versions which are extremely well documented.

This guide will, at all times, attempt to be as thorough as possible, but i can not guarantee it to be understandable 100% of the time. If you have a question and/or don't understand something, don't hesitate to ask.
In General, you have the following help sources:

  1. The seedminer GBATemp Thread
  2. The Seedminer FC and mining help GBATemp Thread
  3. The Nintendo Homebrew Discord
  4. Me, Marenthyu, on Discord.

If you ask for help there, please make sure to tell the persons there that you are coming from this guide, giving them this link.

That being said, i assume you are familiar with at least some degree of terminology regarding Custom Firmware and have read this main page and this getting started page.
Now, you should be here because you could not follow that guide further due to the above mentioned restrictions. If that is indeed the case, then welcome, read on! If NOT, read said guide first and evaluate your options. If none of them are possible for you and you have read it, come back here.

Still here? Good! Then please continue on with the next step.
